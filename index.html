Our Story

Come meet us.

Nyumbani started the way most good things do — with a charcoal grill in a backyard, a pot of pilau on the stove, and too many neighbors showing up for dinner. Josh runs the grill. Aisha runs the kitchen. Between them, they've turned Sunday family cooking into a place the whole block eats at now.

We cook the way we were taught: slow, over real fire, with spice blends passed down rather than measured out. Nothing fancy — just nyama choma done right, ugali that holds its shape, and greens picked up the morning we cook them.

— Josh & Aisha
